Money saving tips on travel food cost

Sometimes food cost might be less predictable than the transportation or accommodation price in your travel planning. The simple solution is to pack a bag of protein bars all the time (just in case) but at the same time save some money for local cuisines! We need a balance! Do some research about the most common food at your destination. Usually those are the cheapest, ranging from fruits, pastry to mains (or beer! In Germany beer might be even cheaper than bottled water!) !
